Kataoka

Poem #252

kiri tachite
kari zo naku naru
kataoka no
ashita no hara wa
momiji shinuramu
Fog and mist hover
and the wild geese are calling:
at Kataoka
Ashita-no-hara plain
the leaves will have turned by now.
