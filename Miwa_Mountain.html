Miwa Mountain

Poem #94

miwayamao
shika mo kakusu ka
harugasumi
hito ni shirarenu
hana ya sakuramu
Observe how the haze
of spring spreads its gauzy mantle
on Miwa Mountain:
might flowers be blooming there
of which men have no knowledge?
