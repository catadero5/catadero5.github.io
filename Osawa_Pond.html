Osawa Pond

Poem #275

ひともとと思ひしきくをおほさはの池のそこにもたれかうゑけむ
hitomoto to
omoishi hana o
osawa no
ike no soko ni mo
tare ka uekemu
A single flower,
so I had thought- but whose hand
might have been the one
to set out a second plant
beneath О̄sawa Pond?
