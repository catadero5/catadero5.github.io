Otowa Mountain

Poem #142

おとは山けさこえくれば郭公こずゑはるかに今ぞなくなる
otowayama
kesa koekureba
hototogisu
kozue haruka ni
ima zo naku naru
Journeying onward
over Otowa Mountain
while the day is young,
I hear a cuckoo singing
high in the distant treetops.

Poem #256

秋風のふきにし日よりおとは山峯のこずゑも色づきにけり
akikaze no
fukinishi hi yori
otowayama
mine no kozue mo
irozukinikeri
Ever since the day
when the autumn wind first blew,
new color has stained
treetops at the very peak
of Otowa Mountain.
